# Vouchy | Your personal voucher manager
Vouchy is an ultimate digital wallet and discount optimizer. Never struggle with calculating which coupons to use again. Just add your vouchers, enter your total bill, and let the app magically find the best combination of discounts to maximize your savings!

Key Features:

🌟 Smart Deal Optimization: 
Enter your total bill amount and select a merchant to let the app instantly calculate the optimal portfolio of vouchers to apply. It provides a clear breakdown of your original bill, total savings, and the new final price.

🌟 Comprehensive Digital Wallet: 
Store all your discount codes in one neatly organized place. The app fully supports both fixed cash vouchers and percentage-based discounts.

🌟 Custom Voucher Details: 
Customize every entry by adding specific merchant names, minimum spend requirements, and start or expiry dates. You can even designate whether a voucher is "stackable" so it can be combined with other offers.

🌟 Effortless Organization: 
Filter your wallet by specific merchants or sort your vouchers by value, creation time, or those expiring soon. The app also automatically groups your expired vouchers into a separate category to keep your active wallet clutter-free.

🌟 Detailed Transaction History: 
Save your optimized deals to keep track of your shopping history over time. You can view detailed digital receipts that break down the exact vouchers applied to your past transactions.

🌟 Voucher Restoration: Need to undo a transaction? You can easily delete a past receipt, which will automatically restore the previously used vouchers right back into your active wallet.
